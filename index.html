      Original name: Romilly Feye
      Occupation: Fashion designer

      At Parsons School of Design, she studied fashion design, womenswear, fashion styling, fashion management, and luxury brand management. In 2019, she returned to Los Angeles and co-founded a fashion design studio with her friend Scarlett Martin, embarking on her entrepreneurial journey. Her designs have won numerous awards, and she has served as design director, fashion marketing director, and design and strategic planning mentor.
